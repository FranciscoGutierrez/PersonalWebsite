Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher @ KU Leuven working on human–computer interaction & information visualization.

My research focuses on visual analytics and communication of uncertainty. Mainly, I am interested in supporting non-expert users in everyday decision-making. Also, I am researching how augmented reality can enhance the user experience on decision support systems.

I follow a design-centered approach that includes a scientific focus on a qualitative and quantitative analysis of behavior. Usually you can find me designing, developing and evaluating interactive visualization systems.

I also like sci-fi, photography, and lots of video games. I have a Youtube channel [TacoLab] with my buddy Sven Charleer.
