About me

I am a 29 years old Mexican doing a Ph.D. in Belgium. My research focuses on visual analytics and communication of uncertainty. Mainly, I am interested in supporting non-expert users on their everyday decision-making. I am also researching how augmented reality can enhance the user experience on decision support systems. My doctoral advisor is Prof. dr. Katrien Verbert.


I follow a design-centered approach that includes a scientific focus on a qualitative and quantitative analysis of behavior. I am teaching assistant of the Data Visualisation course from the Advanced Master of Digital Humanities at KU Leuven. I also like movies, photography, playing guitar, and video games.
