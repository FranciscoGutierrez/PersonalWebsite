About me

I am a 30 years old Mexican doing a PhD in Belgium. My research focuses on visual analytics and communication of uncertainty. Mainly, I am interested in supporting non-expert users in their everyday decision-making. I am also researching how augmented reality can enhance the user experience on decision support systems. My doctoral advisor is Prof. dr. Katrien Verbert.


I follow a design-centered approach that includes a scientific focus on a qualitative and quantitative analysis of behavior. I am a teaching assistant of the Data Visualisation course from the Advanced Master of Digital Humanities at KU Leuven.


I also like sci-fi movies, photography, playing the guitar, going to concerts, playing board games and video games. I proudly have an unsuccesful Youtube channel with my buddy Sven Charleer where we try to do research about eSports and play games.
