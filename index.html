About me

My research focuses on visual analytics and studying uncertainty communication for non-expert users on everyday decision-making. My doctoral advisor is Prof. dr. Katrien Verbert. Recently, I have started researching how augmented reality can enhance the user experience of decision support systems. I follow a user-centered design methodology that includes a scientific focus on qualitative and quantitative analysis of behavior. I am teaching assistant of the Data Visualisation course from the Advanced Master of Digital Humanities at KU Leuven.
